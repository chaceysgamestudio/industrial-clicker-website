Unique Generators:

Instead of standard upgrades, you buy unique generators. You can move them around, and their placement affects performance: far away = more money but slower output, closer = less money but faster. All generators draw power from one central power generator, which produces energy indicated by visible sparks.

Events:

Every 2 minutes (or so), a random event may occur. This could be a protest, a blizzard, or other unpredictable challenges that affect your factory.

Maps:

Unlock maps to get a bird's-eye view of your generators, their layout, and your entire operation.

Power:

Your single power generator fuels all other generators. You can tell it’s working because it emits sparks.

Years:

Time flows automatically—every minute, the year increases by one. Fonts change over time to match the era.
